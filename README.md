ğŸš’ Firefighter Guide
Requirements
You must have the Firefighter job
You must be on duty
Using the Fire Hose
Find a Fire Cart (cart05)
Stand near the cart (within 30 meters)
Type /hose to activate the hose
Aim at fires to spray water
Type /hose again to deactivate
Note: Stay within 30 meters of the cart or the hose will disconnect!

Using a Hydrant
Have a hydrant item in your inventory
Use the hydrant from inventory to place it
Look at the placed hydrant and select "Use Fire Hydrant"
The hydrant will spray water and extinguish nearby fires
You can pick up the hydrant after use
Fire Alerts
When a fire starts:

You will receive a notification
A blip appears on your map
A GPS route guides you to the fire location
The GPS clears when you arrive within 10 meters
Payment
You receive payment for each fire extinguished
Larger structure fires pay a bonus

â“ FAQ
Q: Why can't I use the fire hose?
A: You must be a firefighter and standing near a fire cart.

Q: The fire won't go out!
A: Large fires need multiple attempts. Use the hose or hydrant for better results.

Q: I don't see any fire blips on my map?
A: Make sure you're a firefighter on duty. Some servers only show blips to firefighters.

Q: Why did the fire disappear on its own?
A: Fires have a maximum duration (usually 20 minutes) and will burn out naturally.

Q: Can I pick up the hydrant after placing it?
A: Yes, look at it and select "Pick Up Fire Hydrant".

Q: Do I get paid for fighting fires?
A: Yes, firefighters receive payment for each fire extinguished.
